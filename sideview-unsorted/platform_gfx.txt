This pack includes a fully animated platformer hero as well as an enemy and some tiles/objects. Perfect for for game jams or prototype development.

These were for an old unreleased project, so I am releasing them for free. Have fun!

Here are the individual files that you'll find in the zip file:

Player Sprite Animations:

    Stand
    Run
    Walk
    Jump
    Spinjump
    Fall
    Pivot
    Bonk
    Up
    Down
    Push
    Wall Slide

Enemy Animations:

    Walk
    Die

Tiles:

    3 Block Tiles
    Floating Block
    Crumbling Block (animated)
    Sloped Block
    3 Foliage Tiles (sides and center)
    Spikes

Objects/Collectibles:

    Key
    Food

Enjoy!
