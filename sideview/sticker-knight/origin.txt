This is an open-source prototype of a fantasy themed platformer game. It's not a complete game, but a jumping off point for folks that want to explore and learn modern game programming techniques in Lua with the Corona SDK.

The game, source and graphics are free and free to modify under the Creative Commons CC0 licence.
