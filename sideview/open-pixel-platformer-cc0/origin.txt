Welcome to the Open Pixel Project! We’ve created a set of pixel art game graphics that are completely free to use for any project, all of our assets are in the public domain!

On this website you can browse and download our finished assets, upload your own contributions, and even submit art requests.

You’re very welcome to join our community, please visit us on our discord channel, and post on the forums. If you wish to contribute, the contribution guide and style guide will give you all the information. We’re looking forward to see your work!
